Quick Start Guide

  • Hold Shift + Hover over words to see instant definitions and translations
  • Click words to mark them as known and remove the underline
  • Click "A" button in the popup to instantly add words to Anki
  • Check the banner on the side of pages to see your comprehension percentage
  • Click the Helios icon in your toolbar to view detailed vocabulary statistics
