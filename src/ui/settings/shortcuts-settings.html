💡 Tips:
  • Click any shortcut box and press your desired key combination
  • Shortcuts can include modifier keys (Ctrl, Shift, Alt) or be single keys
  • Shortcuts cannot overlap with each other
  • Shortcuts cannot conflict with Chrome's built-in keyboard shortcuts
  • Common Chrome shortcuts to avoid: Ctrl+W, Ctrl+T, Ctrl+N, F1-F12, etc.
